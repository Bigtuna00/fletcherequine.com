Services

Preventative Care

Preventative care includes physical exams, vaccines, deworming (ask about the new recommended protocol) and often bloodwork.  

Dentistry

I have state of the art dental instruments.  They are water-cooled and vacuum-cleared.  I also offer periodontal diagnosis and treatment.  Periodontal pockets are one of the most painful conditions in the horse’s mouth.

Digital Radiographs

With the latest in digital technology, I’m sure to get high-quality radiographs for you.

Digital Ultrasound

Diagnosing soft tissue problems and creating an individual rehab program for each horse is so important to proper healing.  Imaging of a horse’s legs, neck, back, SI, abdomen and thorax is available.

Ophthalmology

Eye issues can be serious, please don’t wait if your horse’s eye is painful!  I have an extensive education in eye issues and really enjoy helping your horse heal from their eye problems.

Lameness Exams

Thorough lameness exams can help figure out the source of your horse’s pain or poor performance.  IRAP, PRP, ProStride, Shockwave and Stem Cells available.

Pre-Purchase Exams

Thorough and fair Pre-Purchase exams will give any buyer a good idea of what they are purchasing.

Geriatric Care

The older horse is so special!  They have served us for many years and deserve the best we can offer.  Their medical needs change as they age and I can help you make sure they are getting the best care possible.
